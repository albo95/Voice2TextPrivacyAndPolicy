Privacy Policy for Voice2text

Last Updated: 11/09/2024

1. No Collection of User Data

Voice2text does not collect, store, or process any personal data from its users. Our app is designed to transcribe WhatsApp voice messages without accessing, storing, or sharing any of your personal information.

2. Use of RevenueCat

Voice2text uses RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. While Voice2text does not collect any personal data, RevenueCat may collect certain information necessary to process subscriptions and payments. This information may include:

  • Transaction data (e.g., purchase history)
  • Device identifiers

Please refer to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for more details on how they handle your data.

3. Third-Party Services

Aside from RevenueCat, Voice2text does not use any other third-party services that may collect personal data.

4. User Rights

Since Voice2text does not collect any personal data, there is no need for data access, correction, or deletion requests. However, if you have any concerns regarding the data handled by RevenueCat, we encourage you to contact them directly or review their privacy policy.
